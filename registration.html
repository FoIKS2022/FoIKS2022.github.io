FoIKS 2022 Registration

To register to FoIKS 2020, please use the online registration form available at https://onlinepayments.it.helsinki.fi/product/12th-international-symposium-on-foundations-of-information-and-knowledge-systems/ .

Registration to FoIKS 2022 will be possible until June 13. However, we recommend registering by May 20 to benefit from the lower early registration fees. Note that in order to qualify for student registration, proof of student status (copy of student card, letter from supervising Professor, or similar) needs to be sent to foiks22local@gmail.com.

The registration fees are as follows, including all invited talks and contributed talk sessions during June 20-23, coffee breaks and lunched during June 20-23, an opening reception on June 20, and a combined excursion and conference dinner on June 22.
